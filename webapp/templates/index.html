Skills Mapping Console

Job market skills matched to ECSF using SBERT.

View mappings

What is shown

The mapping view lists each normalized skill with its top ECSF matches and similarity scores. Filters let you control the top-k and threshold.

Inputs

Skill list from job postings and ECSF TKS text.

Model

SentenceTransformer SBERT (all-MiniLM-L6-v2).

Output

Interpretable top-k matches stored in a SQL view.
